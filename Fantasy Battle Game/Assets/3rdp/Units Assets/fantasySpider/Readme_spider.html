Fantasy Spider

This spider is an older work of mine.  Instead of gathering dust on my hard drive, I decided to share it with unity people. It was already
downloadable from TurboSquid for quite some time.

There is not much to tell about this spider, its animations are already set and ready to play (it uses the legacy system, but I think you can 
easily convert it to mechanim).

Its material is cutout transparent, two-sided. If you use the spider as a small object, you might want to change that to a simple diffuse
to avoid alpha overdraws, etc.
